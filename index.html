About Me

I'm currently in the process of making a career transition into web development. Until the end of 2019 I was a self employed marine spray-painter, but I decided that that was not a fulfilling or mentally engaging enough career so after some investigation and advice I settled on web development as the path forward for me. To make that happen in the first half of 2020 I studied with Enspiral Dev Academy, a full immersion work simulation full stack web development bootcamp. I went from having no knowledge or experience with code to writing full stack JavaScript applications over the course of fifteen weeks, while also learning and applying agile methodologies and team coding practices. In the course of this experience I discovered in myself a joy in solving problems, making things work and writing beautiful, functional code.

Now I am trying to take the final step into my new career by finding a position in the industry from which I can can continue to grow as a developer. In the meantime I am expanding my skillset and working on various projects.

When I'm not coding I like to read about science and history, paint models and play guitar.
